Help Us Help You Create Great Content

We want to elevate the voice of many people in the data community, to encourage and help them share their stories and opinions about data mesh. We want to help you find content ideas that will be engaging and also give you access to friendly volunteers who may review your content prior to publishing to give you their thoughts. We do not guarantee any one will review your content but we want to set you up to be successful.

As part of our drive to help create awesome data mesh content, we have a content "wish list" that you can find here. We are working on better ways to vote on what content people want to see most but our current system could use some work. If you want to help with that, we'd love it, send us an email.

The current most requested content is around:

1) getting buy-in as "X" with X being different roles

2) initial going, e.g. is it all or nothing, can we just create a single data product first, etc.

3) common mistakes and pitfalls to avoid

4) user stories

Help us define a better user story template. We have a default template here but it can always be better. You can directly comment on the doc, submit annoymous feedback through our form here, or email us at {email}.
